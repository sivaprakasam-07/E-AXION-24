SILICON SAGA

Conquer the Tech World in Silicon Saga!

Welcome to SILICON SAGA, a thrilling quiz adventure that challenges your knowledge and quick thinking in the field of electronics and technology. Form your team and get ready to compete in three exciting rounds.

Guidelines:

1.Each team should consist of maximum 2 members.
2.Strict adherence to time limits is required.
3.The competition comprises 3 rounds.
4.The judges decisions are final.

About the Event:

• Round 1: Pen and Paper-Kick off the adventure with a written test featuring technical questions. Sharpen your knowledge and speed to rank high in this foundational round.
• Round 2: Crossword on Technical Questions-Move on to a challenging crossword round with advanced technical questions. This round tests your understanding and ability to think critically under pressure.
• Round 3: Rapid Fire-Conclude with a rapid-fire question round, where you must answer advanced level questions quickly and accurately. Showcase your knowledge and quick thinking skills to win the competition.
