BRAIN BUSTERS

Unlock the Genius Within: Conquer the BRAIN BUSTERS Challenge!

Description:

Welcome to BRAIN BUSTERS, the ultimate test of knowledge, skill, and quick thinking in the world of electronics and technology! Gather your team and prepare for an exhilarating journey through three intense rounds designed to challenge your intellect and creativity.

Guidelines:

1.Each team must consist of 2 members.
2.Strict adherence to time limits is required.
3.The competition comprises 3 rounds.

About the Event:

• Round 1: Jigsaw Puzzles-Dive into a written test where you will solve complex puzzles related to electronics and technology. Only the sharpest minds will advance to the next round.
• Round 2: Electro Quest-Face an oral round where speed and accuracy are key. Participants will be eliminated based on their response times, pushing you to think and react swiftly under pressure.
• Round 3: Mind Play-Conclude with a PPT-based Q&A round, where your presentation skills and depth of knowledge will be tested. Showcase your expertise and outshine your competitors to emerge victorious.
