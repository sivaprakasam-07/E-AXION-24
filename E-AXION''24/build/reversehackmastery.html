REVERSE HACK MASTERY

Master the Art of Reverse Engineering: Conquer REVERSE HACK MASTERY!

Description:

Welcome to REVERSE HACK MASTERY, a competition designed to test your problem-solving, debugging, and reverse engineering skills. Form your team and get ready to dive into three challenging rounds that will push your technical abilities to the limit.

Guidelines:

1.Each team must consist of 2 or 3 members.
2.Strict adherence to time limits is required.
3.The competition comprises 3 rounds.
4.The judges decisions are final.

About the Event:

• Round 1: Identify and Connect Participants will be given clues to identify relevant product names, circuit types, and electronic terms. Quick thinking and sharp identification skills are essential to advance to the next round.
• Round 2: Fix-the-Bug Teams will receive a circuit image and software code. Your task is to debug both the circuit and the code to produce the correct output. This round tests your troubleshooting and coding skills under pressure.
• Round 3: Reversing the Hack Study an electronic product to reverse engineer and document its specifications and manufacturing details. Present a detailed report on how to design a replica of the product to the judges, showcasing your in-depth understanding and analytical skills.
