TECHNO HUNT

Embark on the Ultimate TECH QUEST: Join the TECHNO HUNT!

Description:

Welcome to TECHNO HUNT, where your technical prowess and quick thinking will be put to the ultimate test! Form your team and dive into an electrifying competition designed to challenge your knowledge and speed.

Guidelines:

1. Each team must consist of 2 or 3 members.
2. Strict adherence to time limits is required.
3. The competition comprises 2 rounds.
4. The judges decisions are final.

About the Event:

• Round 1: Rapid Fire Technical-Q&A Teams will face a series of rapid-fire technical questions, with their ranking determined by the number of correct answers they can provide within one minute. Only the top teams will advance to the next round.
• Round 2: Technical Term Hunt-The selected teams will embark on a hunt for technical terms based on given descriptions. Quick thinking and a sharp mind are crucial to succeed in this thrilling round.
